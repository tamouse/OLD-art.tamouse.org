Pages

This area can be used for static web site pages that aren’t blog posts, such as a colophon, an “about me” or “about this blog” page, collections of information, documentation, notes, etc.

It’s certainly possible to make your entire site about non-blog static pages as well, such as a recipe / cookbook site, or a knowledge base, or even a replacement for a wiki.

The navbar front matter page variable determines whether this page will be included in the navigation bar on the site. Omit it or set it to false and it won’t show up.

A good practice here is to provide a table of contents into the rest of the pages.
