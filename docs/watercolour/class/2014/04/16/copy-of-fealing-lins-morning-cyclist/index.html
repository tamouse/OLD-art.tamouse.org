Copying “Morning Cyclist” by Fealing Lin

We’ve admired the works of Fealing Lin in class for some time. As a recent subject of an article in Watercolor Artist magazine, and our teacher attending a workshop with Ms. Lin, we wanted to study more of her work.

This is “Morning Cyclist” which has a dreamy, etherical quality, and an amazing use of very wet and very bright colours.
