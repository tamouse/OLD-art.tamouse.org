With Sample
full size


click on image for slideshow

In my initial analysis, trying to write music with this pen/nib combination would drive me crazy because it’s way too wet and uncontrollable. Although I think it’s really the prettiest pen in my collection right now, right up there with the MV Intima Glacier Blue, it’s probably not going into regular rotation anytime soon.

Overall, I’m a bit disapointed, but I’m still going to keep it.
