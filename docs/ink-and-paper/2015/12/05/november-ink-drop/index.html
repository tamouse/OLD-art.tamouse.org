All-in-all a very nice collection.

Interestingly (maybe) is that I like all the non-blues a lot. I can’t decide which of them I should get!

There was a slight mix-up in my October ink drop: an extra vial of Heart of Darkness was in a vial labeled “Noodler’s Nightshade”. I liked the Nightshade, so I ordered a bottle. When it arrived, it looked quite different. After a few emails back and forth, we figured out what was mislabeled.

The Goulet’s were awfully nice and sent me two vials of the HoD (which was backordered at the time) and threw in two more vials: one of Onyx Black (which I already own and love) and one of Private Reserve Black Magic Blue.

Awfully sweet, those Goulet’s (hat tip to Ross at GP Customer Service! well done!).
